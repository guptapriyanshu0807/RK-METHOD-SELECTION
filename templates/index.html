Choose the best RK method for your ODE.

Enter a first-order ODE in terms of t and y. The app benchmarks the standard RK methods, feeds the generated feature vector into the trained model, and returns the predicted best method with the computed solution curve.

You can also provide an exact solution. If you leave that field blank, the app uses the built-in RK10 reference path to estimate the numerical error.
